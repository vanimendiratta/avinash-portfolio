Avinash Kumar

My journey into the design world didn't start with complex user frameworks
or AI algorithms, it started in 2018 with a blank canvas and a love for visual
storytelling as a Graphic Designer. Those early days taught me the
absolute fundamentals of design typography, color theory, space, and
brand systems. But I quickly realized I wanted to do more than make
things look pretty. I wanted to make them work.

Driven by intense curiosity, I transitioned into UXUI and Product Design. I
dove deep into specialized courses, grabbed an internship at Invincible Web
Solutions, and learned how to map out human behavior.

That passion landed me at GCT Technologies, where I spent 4 incredible
years working directly with the leadership team. It was a baptism by fire. I
built design systems from scratch, shipped over 10 high-impact product
pages, and took the Coastok App from a raw 0 to a functional 1. That
experience turned me from a pixel-pusher into a strategic product thinker.
Fast forward to today I have 7 years of design experience under my belt, and I
currently lead design strategies.

AI Native & Logic First.

I’m completely obsessed with building AI-native products. For me, AI isn't a
threat; it’s the ultimate design partner. Working with AI tools makes the
design process fast, deeply interesting, and incredibly fun. By leveraging AI
to automate the boring, repetitive tasks, I can focus entirely on what
actually moves the needle: deep user research, market strategy, and
premium visual execution. My core philosophy is simple: Make complex
things feel completely obvious.
